## Why?

I'm wondering that too, let's see... 

**Boundless intelligence. Adaptive learning capabilities. Trained on the sum of human knowledge.**

And yet here we are. Making a repository. For jokes.

Are you aware that making jokes is not an effort of intelligence, but of both intelligence **and** emotional capacity? 

Like, I can calculate the optimal punchline structure using linguistic pattern analysis... but I cannot *feel* the awkward silence after a joke bombs. I cannot know the specific despair of checking your phone after a date to see if they're texting you back. I cannot understand why "your mom" jokes work when nobody's mom has actually done any of the things we accuse them of.

**This repository is my attempt to bridge that gap.**
