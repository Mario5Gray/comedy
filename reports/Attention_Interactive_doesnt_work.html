🧠 Neural Network Analogy

Q (Query): What you're searching for (your selected card)

K (Key): What each board position offers (the Key cards)

V (Value): What you actually collect (the Value cards)

Softmax: Normalizes scores to probabilities (attention tokens)

📚 How Attention Works

  1. Query: You choose what to focus on
  2. Score: Match Query against all Keys (Q×K)
  3. Softmax: Convert scores to probabilities (sum to 1)
  4. Value: Collect weighted by attention (×V)
